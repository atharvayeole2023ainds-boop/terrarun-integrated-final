# Privacy Policy for TerraRun

**Effective Date:** May 2024

TerraRun is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect and use your data.

### 1. Data We Collect
- **Location Data**: We collect real-time GPS location data during active "Missions" to calculate distance and verify territory captures.
- **Account Information**: Email, username, and profile data provided during registration.
- **Activity Data**: Logs of your runs and cycles, including tracks and metrics.

### 2. How We Use Data
- To provide the core gameplay experience (territory mapping).
- To maintain leaderboards and social features.
- To prevent cheating and ensure fair play.

### 3. Data Sharing
- Your username and public profile data are visible to other players.
- GPS tracks are public by default but can be set to private.
- We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

### 4. Your Rights
You can request to delete your account and associated data at any time through the app settings or by contacting support.
